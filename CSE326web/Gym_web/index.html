What we Provide

Cardio

Cardio exercise, which is sometimes referred to as aerobic exercise, is any rhythmic activity that raises your heart rate into your target heart rate zone. This is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories

Resistance Training

Resistance training increases muscle strength by making your muscles work against a weight or force.

Weight Training

Weight training is a type of strength training that uses weights for resistance. Weight training provides a stress to the muscles that causes them to adapt and get stronger, similar to the way aerobic conditioning strengthens your heart.

Tabata Training

Tabata training breaks a workout down into clearly defined intervals â€“ typically, 20 seconds of a push-it-to-the-limit exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest

Zumba Training

Zumba is a fitness program that combines elements of salsa, merengue, samba, reggaeton, and hip-hop with other aerobic exercises.

Advanced Training

Advanced trainees use advanced exercises that challenge their bodies for increased strength, power, speed, and extra intensity to achieve added results
